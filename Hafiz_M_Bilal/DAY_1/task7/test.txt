Test Cases
Case 1: Bitwise operations

Input: 

x = 5, y = 9


Output:

x = 5, y = 9
x & y = 1
x | y = 13
x ^ y = 12
~x = -6
~y = -10
x << 1 = 10
y << 1 = 18
x >> 1 = 2
y >> 1 = 4

16 is a power of 2


Input:
